十分建築關注人、空間與自然之間的關係。 我們相信建築不僅是遮蔽的容器，而是一種能與光線、風、時間共呼吸的存在。 在每個場域中，我們試圖理解土地的語言——氣候、材質、聲音與生活的節奏， 以節制而誠實的手法，讓建築成為人與自然交流的媒介。 我們重視日常的溫度，也珍視「留白」所帶來的靜謐。 那是一種不以形式取勝，而以空氣與時間滲透的設計態度。 我們相信，真正的空間不在於填滿，而在於保留能呼吸的餘地， 讓人能於其中感受光影的移動、風的流動、生活的節奏。 建築在此不為主角，而是讓人安心、讓自然滲入的背景。 由王喆建築師創立的十分建築，持續探索建築在現代生活中的靜謐角色。 我們相信，真正的建築並非形象，而是當人駐足時，那股可以呼吸的平靜。

Very Studio | Che Wang Architects explores the relationship between people, space, and nature. We believe architecture is not merely a shelter, but a presence that breathes with light, wind, and time. In every site, we seek to understand the language of the land—its climate, texture, sound, and rhythm of life— through restrained and honest gestures, allowing architecture to become a medium between humans and nature. We value the warmth of the everyday, and we embrace the idea of ma—the art of emptiness and pause. Rather than filling space, we allow it to breathe; rather than seeking form, we seek stillness. True architecture, we believe, lies in the intervals—the unspoken margins where light shifts, wind passes, and life quietly unfolds. Here, architecture is not the protagonist, but a calm background that lets people rest and nature flow through. Founded by architect Che Wang, Very Studio continues to explore the silent, breathing role of architecture in modern life. We believe that genuine architecture is not an image, but a moment of stillness that breathes when one pauses.
